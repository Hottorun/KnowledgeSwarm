Acme Corp signed a supply agreement with VoltGrid GmbH.
VoltGrid GmbH provides grid integration services to Acme Corp.
Acme Corp pays VoltGrid GmbH a monthly integration fee of 50000 USD.
Acme Corp requires VoltGrid GmbH to support GridMind deployments for three years.
Acme Corp will use Beta Storage components in the first VoltGrid GmbH deployment.
Jonas Weber manages the VoltGrid GmbH partnership for Acme Corp.
