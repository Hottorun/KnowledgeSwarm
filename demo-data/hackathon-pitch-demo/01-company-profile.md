NovaCore Robotics is an industrial robotics company based in Boston, Massachusetts.
NovaCore Robotics builds warehouse automation robots for logistics companies and manufacturers.
NovaCore Robotics sells the AtlasPick robot arm and the FleetMind orchestration platform.
NovaCore Robotics serves customers in the United States, Germany, and Singapore.
NovaCore Robotics is led by CEO Amara Singh.
NovaCore Robotics reports to a five-member board chaired by Victor Lane.
NovaCore Robotics owns the product roadmap for AtlasPick and FleetMind.
NovaCore Robotics depends on precision sensors, battery packs, robotic grippers, and cloud scheduling software.
